Bellgold Travel Consult is a fast growing and reliable immigration-travel consultancy firm in Nigeria. Bellgold Travels has established itself as an integral part of the aviation and immigration industry in Nigeria, with an integrated range of travel services orientated to consistently enhance the satisfaction of its clients. Bellgold Travels has continued to develop and gain more ground on increased patronage from organized corporate bodies, academic institutions, groups and individuals because its value system is hinged on professionalism, advance technology, integrity, ethics and superior customer service. It maintains a culture of excellence and goes to great lengths to achieve the company popular phrase; ‘service with professionalism and integrity to your satisfaction’. The consultancy operates a very competitive environment, where client can learn its corporate culture and apply them in all they do. In addition it operates an open door policy to foster a feeling of equality amongst staff and ensure everyone is accessible at all times.

OUR SHARED VALUES

  • We are a customer - oriented service institution.
  • Our people, our community and the physical environment are the core constituents of our value system.
  • Our goal is to build a culture of excellence in collaboration/partnership with like – minded institutions and organization in Nigeria and around the world.

OUR MISSION

To provide quality, selfless and passionate service to our client with the aid of advance technology, informative ideas and highly motivated workforce using professionalism and integrity to achieve customer satisfaction.

To contribute to the success of any Nigerian citizen who desire to travel out of the country, thereby serving as a leading immigration and travel company in Nigeria.

OUR CORE VALUES

Integrity, Excellent, Accessible, Innovative, Passionate-efficiency, Result-driven and Professionalism.

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE HIRING US

  • We don't operate the way regular Nigerian Travel agent does, rather we operate the way International Immigration/Travel Company Abroad does.
  • Our Services are contracted upon a set consultancy service fee, which is affordable to the average Nigerian and usually payable in three installments.
  • We have an Associate-link with a leading Canadian Immigration Lawyer for Immigration Back-up services.
  • We don't hoard information from our client rather we see every client as a Bellgold Travels team-member.
  • Our Financial dealings with all clients is transparent, as we give out pro-forma invoice to client before engaging our services.
  • We have a free six-weeks intensive IELTS coaching package to our clients through our partnering recognized IELTS Training Institute.
  • We assist our clients in running their personal bank account with required funds for visa approvals through our partnering financial institutions.
  • Our Immigration and Travel Consultants are Honest, Reliable and Professionals.
